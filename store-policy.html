Store Policy

Customer Care

At Ayiwa Reigns Clothing, we prioritize our customers and strive to deliver the highest level of service. Please note that all payments made are non-refundable. We are committed to ensuring that you are satisfied with our services, so we provide detailed consultations and updates throughout the design and production process.

  • Appointment Booking: A non-refundable commitment fee of 500 GHC is required to secure your appointment. This fee will be deducted from your final payment once we agree on the final design.
  • Deposit Policy: A 70% deposit is required before we begin any work. This deposit allows us to source the finest materials and begin production.
  • Design Timeline: Please allow at least one month for sketches. This ensures that each design is given the care and attention it deserves, reflecting your unique vision.
